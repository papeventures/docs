MediaOS includes a full suite of built-in reports that cover every part of your ad sales and billing operation, from pipeline forecasting to payment allocation and invoice aging. Reports are accessible from the **Reports** section of the main navigation. This guide covers the available report types, how to configure and run them, and how to share or export results.

## Available built-in reports

## How to run a report

1. Navigate to **Reports** in the main navigation and select the report you want to run.
2. Use the filter controls at the top of the report page to narrow the results. Common filters include:
   - **Date range** — most reports let you select a from/to date or choose a relative period (this month, last quarter, next 90 days, etc.)
   - **Product / product type** — limit results to a specific publication, ad type, or product category
   - **Sales rep** — filter by the user who sold the contract or who owns the account
   - **Advertiser** — run the report for a single account
   - **Contract or insertion status** — include only sold contracts, open contracts, or specific pipeline statuses
3. Click **Filter** or **Search** to apply your selections and load the results.

<Tip>
  The Year-to-Date Comparison report has an additional **Comparison Year** option. MediaOS automatically calculates the matching "contracts sold as of" date for the prior year so the comparison is apples-to-apples.
</Tip>

## Sales forecasting (advanced)

The **Advanced Forecasting** report is available to admins and sales managers on the Plus plan or higher. Access it from **Sales → Forecast → Advanced Forecasting**.

This report displays a set of stacked bar charts showing:

- Pipeline forecast by deal stage over time
- Forecasted revenue by product and by month
- Page count projections by ad size and by rep
- Revenue breakdown by product type and pipeline status

You can filter by date range, breakdown period (month, quarter, or year), sales rep, and specific products. Charts are rendered interactively and can be exported.

## Custom reports

For reporting needs beyond the built-in templates, MediaOS provides a custom report builder. From the Reports section, look for the **Custom Reports** option to:

- Choose a base data set (contracts, insertions, invoices, payments, or accounts)
- Select which columns to include
- Apply filters and groupings
- Save the configuration as a named report view that you can return to later

<Note>
  Custom report access and the ability to save report views may depend on your plan level and user role. Contact your account administrator if you do not see the custom report option.
</Note>

## Sharing reports

Reports in MediaOS can be shared with other users in your organization in two ways:

- **Saved filters** — many reports support saving a specific filter configuration (date range, product, user, etc.) as a named tab that appears at the top of the report page. These saved filter tabs are visible to all users with report access.
- **Exported links** — you can copy the URL of a filtered report and send it to a colleague. As long as they are logged in to MediaOS, they will see the same filtered view.

## Exporting report data

Most built-in reports include an **Export CSV** button in the toolbar. Click it to download the current filtered report results as a comma-separated values file suitable for use in Excel or other tools.

The Sales Run Sheet and Activity report also offer a **Print** option that formats the report for printing or saving as a PDF directly from your browser.

<Warning>
  CSV export for some reports is restricted to admins, accounting users, and sales managers. If you do not see the Export button, your user role may not have permission.
</Warning>

## Dashboard widgets

The MediaOS dashboard surfaces key metrics from your reports without requiring you to navigate to the full report view. Common dashboard widgets include:

- **Revenue summary** — total invoiced, total collected, and outstanding balance for the current period
- **Pipeline summary** — deal values by stage for the current month or quarter
- **Recent activity** — the latest notes, tasks, and workflow events across your accounts
- **Upcoming insertions** — a preview of the run sheet for the next two weeks

Dashboard widgets can be configured per user, so each team member sees the numbers most relevant to their role.
