MediaOS is built for media companies that sell advertising. It gives your sales team a single place to manage every step of the ad sales process—from prospecting and proposals to contract signing, ad delivery, and invoicing. Whether you run a print publication, digital media property, or a combination of both, MediaOS connects your CRM, order management, and accounting workflows so nothing falls through the cracks.

## Key features

### Sales pipeline

Track every deal from first contact to closed sale. MediaOS gives you a visual pipeline where you can move opportunities through custom stages, assign reps, set follow-up tasks, and see your entire book of business at a glance.

### Contracts and proposals

Create professional proposals directly from your product catalog. Send them to advertisers for review, collect e-signatures via DocuSign, and automatically convert signed proposals into contracts—all without leaving MediaOS.

### Invoicing and billing

Generate invoices from contracts automatically. MediaOS supports one-time and recurring billing schedules, payment plans, partial payments, and automated payment reminders. Tax rates are applied per line item based on the rules you configure.

### Ad delivery tracking

Track the status of every insertion in your schedule. Upload artwork, manage proofs, record delivery confirmations, and flag issues—all tied to the originating contract line item.

### Reporting

Built-in reports give you visibility into revenue by rep, advertiser, publication, issue, and date range. Export to CSV or use the API to pull data into your own analytics tools.

### Automation and workflows

Automate repetitive tasks with MediaOS workflows. Trigger actions—like sending a follow-up email, posting to Slack, or pushing a task to Monday.com—based on contract status changes, payment events, or pipeline stage updates.

### Integrations

MediaOS connects with the tools your team already uses:

- **QuickBooks** — Bi-directional sync of invoices, payments, and contacts (Plus plan and above)
- **DocuSign** — Send proposals for e-signature; signed documents convert to contracts automatically (Plus plan and above)
- **HubSpot** — Import contacts and companies; view MediaOS contracts and invoices inside HubSpot (Premium)
- **Zapier** — Connect MediaOS to over 1,000 apps using triggers and actions (Premium)
- **Stripe** — Accept credit card payments online (all plans)
- **Authorize.Net** — Store cards on file and process payments (Standard and above)
- **Google Ad Manager** — Book inventory and sync orders directly from MediaOS (Premium)
- **Mailchimp** — Sync advertiser contacts to your email lists (Standard and above)
- **Broadstreet Ads** — Push sales to Broadstreet and pull billing details automatically (Premium)
- **SendGrid** — Use your own email relay for improved deliverability (Premium)
- **Slack** — Post workflow notifications to channels (Premium)
- **RingCentral** — Click-to-dial and automatic call log sync (Premium)
- **Twilio** — Integrated auto-dialer (Premium)
- **Lob** — Automate print marketing and invoice mailings (Premium)
- **Monday.com** — Push tasks via workflows (Premium)
- **Adobe InDesign** — Pull runsheets and artwork directly into InDesign layouts (Premium)

### REST API

The MediaOS REST API gives you programmatic access to advertisers, agencies, contacts, contracts, invoices, payments, products, and ad requests. API access requires a Premium plan and an administrator account. See the [API authentication guide](/api/authentication) to get started.
