MediaOS connects to a wide range of third-party tools so your ad sales workflow stays in sync with the rest of your business. You can find every integration by navigating to **Settings > Integrations** in your MediaOS account. Each integration has its own configuration panel where you enter credentials or authorize a connection.

## Integration categories

<CardGroup cols={3}>
  <Card title="Accounting" icon="calculator" href="/integrations/quickbooks">
    **QuickBooks** — Sync invoices, payments, and contact information bidirectionally with QuickBooks Online.
  </Card>
  <Card title="CRM" icon="address-book" href="/integrations/hubspot">
    **HubSpot** — Import contacts and companies into MediaOS and display contracts, proposals, and invoices inside HubSpot.
  </Card>
  <Card title="E-signature" icon="file-signature" href="/integrations/docusign">
    **DocuSign** — Send proposals for digital signature. Upon signing, the proposal automatically converts to a contract.
  </Card>
  <Card title="Payments" icon="credit-card">
    **Stripe** — Process credit cards through Stripe.com (Starter plan and above).

    **Authorize.Net** — Store cards on file and accept credit cards via Authorize.net API credentials (Standard plan and above).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Communication" icon="comments">
    **Slack** — Post messages from MediaOS into Slack channels. Works with MediaOS Workflows.

    **Twilio** — Fully integrated auto-dialer system (Premium plan).

    **RingCentral** — Click-to-dial on desk phones and softphones; syncs call logs to advertiser activity feeds (Premium plan).

    **SendGrid** — Use your own email relay to improve deliverability (Premium plan).
  </Card>
  <Card title="Automation" icon="bolt" href="/integrations/zapier">
    **Zapier** — Connect MediaOS to 1,000+ other apps via Zapier's automation platform (Premium plan).
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

## All integrations

|---|---|---|---|
| Authorize.Net | Payments | Standard | Accept and store credit cards via Authorize.net. |
| Adobe InDesign | Production | Premium | Pull artwork and runsheets directly into InDesign. |
| Broadstreet Ads | Ad serving | Premium | Push sales to Broadstreet's ad server automatically. |
| DocuSign | E-signature | Plus | Send proposals for e-signature; converts to contract on signing. |
| Google Ad Manager | Ad serving | Premium | Book Google Ad Manager inventory directly from proposals. |
| HubSpot | CRM | Premium | Import contacts and companies; show MediaOS data in HubSpot. |
| Lob | Direct mail | Premium | Automate print marketing and invoice mailings. |
| Mailchimp | Email marketing | Standard | Sync advertiser contacts to your Mailchimp account. |
| Media Radar | Research | Premium | Access Media Radar competitive intelligence data. |
| Monday.com | Project management | Premium | Push tasks to Monday.com via workflows. |
| Phone | CRM | Standard | Open a contact's CRM page automatically on incoming calls. |
| QuickBooks | Accounting | Plus | Bidirectional sync of invoices, payments, and contacts. |
| RingCentral | Communication | Premium | Click-to-dial and automatic call log sync. |
| SendGrid | Email relay | Premium | Route MediaOS emails through your own SendGrid account. |
| Slack | Communication | Premium | Post notifications to Slack channels from workflows. |
| Stripe | Payments | Starter | Process credit cards via Stripe. |
| Subscription Genius | Circulation | Starter | Manage subscriptions and complimentary subscriptions. |
| Twilio | Communication | Premium | Auto-dialer integrated with MediaOS. |
| Zapier | Automation | Premium | Connect MediaOS to 1,000+ apps. |

<Note>
  Plan requirements shown above are minimums. Some features within an integration may require a higher plan tier. Contact your MediaOS account manager if you are unsure which plan you are on.
